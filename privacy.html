Privacy Policy

Ante Up Golf — Last updated: April 13, 2026

This privacy policy describes how Ante Up Golf ("Ante Up", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile app on iOS or Android.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Display name (required) — shown to other players in your rounds
  • Phone number (optional, collected after your first round) — used only for account recovery; never shared with other players or third parties
  • Profile photo (optional) — you choose an image from your device's photo library

Ante Up uses anonymous authentication by default. You are not required to provide an email address to use the app.

Golf Round Data

  • Round titles, course selections, scores, and side-game details
  • GPS-derived data used during live rounds: current hole, distances to fairway and green, shot history for the current round
  • Green location confirmations you submit to improve course data (optional, anonymized)

Location Data

  • Precise location (GPS) is used while the app is in use to determine which hole you're on, show distances to targets, and auto-advance holes
  • Location is not tracked in the background and is not stored on our servers beyond the active round
  • You may deny or revoke location access at any time in your device settings; the scorecard and betting features will still work without it

Contacts

  • If you choose to invite friends via your phone's contact list, Ante Up reads your contacts only during the invite action and only to display them to you
  • Contacts are never uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties

Payment Method Details (Settlement)

  • When you voluntarily add a Venmo username, Zelle phone number, or other payment identifier, we store it so other players in your round know how to settle up with you
  • Ante Up does not process, hold, transfer, or facilitate any money or funds. All settlement happens between players via external payment apps or in person.

Purchases

If you purchase a subscription or in-app product, payment is processed by Apple or Google. We receive a receipt via RevenueCat (our subscription management provider) to unlock your features. We never see or store your payment card details.

Diagnostic Data

Anonymous crash reports and performance metrics via Sentry, to help us fix bugs.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide core app features: round coordination, scoring, live GPS distances, side-game tracking, and settlement display
  • To show your name, scores, and (if provided) payment contact info to other players in rounds you join
  • To diagnose crashes and improve app stability
  • To communicate service-critical changes (e.g., terms updates)

What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not process, hold, or transfer money
  • We do not sell your personal data
  • We do not use your data for advertising or build advertising profiles
  • We do not share your data with anyone outside the rounds you participate in
  • We do not track your location in the background

Data Sharing Within the App

Other players in your round can see:

  • Your display name and profile photo
  • Your scores and side-game results
  • Your payment contact info (only if you've added it, and typically only shown to players settling with you)
  • Your round participation status (ready, agreed, etc.)

Data Storage and Security

  • Data is stored on Supabase (PostgreSQL) with encryption at rest and in transit (TLS)
  • Authentication tokens are stored on-device using platform-secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore)
  • Access to production data is restricted to the developer

Data Retention

  • Account data: retained while your account is active, and up to 30 days after deletion request
  • Round data: retained indefinitely as part of your personal round history unless you request deletion
  • Anonymous course-geometry confirmations: retained indefinitely (they contain no personal identifiers)
  • Crash reports: retained by Sentry per their standard policy (typically 90 days)

Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all your data inside the app
  • Correction: You can update your profile at any time
  • Deletion: Email cwegeleben@gmail.com with the subject "Delete My Account" and we will delete your account and associated personal data within 30 days
  • Withdraw permissions: You can revoke location, contacts, or photo access at any time in your device settings

Children

Ante Up is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

International Users

Ante Up is operated from the United States. If you use the app from outside the US, you consent to your data being processed in the US.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced in the app and reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
