Community Guidelines

Ante Up Golf — Last updated: April 19, 2026

Ante Up is built for friends playing golf together. Keep it that way.

Be respectful

  • No harassment, bullying, or personal attacks
  • No hate speech or discriminatory language
  • No threats of violence

Keep it clean

  • No spam or advertising
  • No sexually explicit content
  • No sharing of others' personal information without consent

Play fair

  • No intentionally false scores or manipulated results
  • No impersonation of other players

What happens if you break the rules

We may remove messages, suspend chat access, or disable accounts that violate these guidelines. If you see something that doesn't belong, tap the message and use the Report button.
