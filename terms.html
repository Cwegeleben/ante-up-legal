1. What Ante Up Is

Ante Up is a coordination tool for private golf rounds. It helps groups of players:

  • Create and join golf rounds
  • Agree on side games and stakes before playing
  • Track scores during the round
  • Track settlement status after the round

2. What Ante Up Is NOT

Ante Up is not a gambling platform, payment processor, or financial service.

  • No money is wagered, held, processed, or transferred through this app.
  • No escrow, wallet, or payment functionality exists.
  • All settlements are handled directly between players outside the app using their preferred payment methods (Venmo, Zelle, cash, etc.).
  • Ante Up only tracks who owes whom and whether payments have been self-reported as completed.
